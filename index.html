    About Us

    With over 25 years of experience, TECHNIKA has built an extensive and diverse portfolio in the field of structural engineering offering high-quality technical services. The basis of the company’s philosophy is through team spirit, professionalism, and continuous enhancement our engineers’ technical expertise to serve client objectives.

    Extensive Experience

    TECHNIKA has build an extensive portfolio in structural engineering, delivering high-quality technical services and demostrating commitment to excellence.

    Core Philosophies

    Guided by core principles of team spirit and professionalism, TECHNIKA continually enhances engineers' technical expertise, embodying a philosophy dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in every project.

    Client-Centric Approach

    TECHNIKA's client-centric approach revolves around understanding and serving client objectives, ensuring that our engineers' technical competence is tailored to exceed expectations and deliver exceptional results.

    Our Mission

    At the heart of our mission is client satisfaction in structural solutions. We specialize in detailed assessments of existing structures, tailoring rehabilitation measures to client needs. Our passion is crafting imaginative designs for new structures, merging creativity and precision. We set ourselves apart by integrating cutting-edge methods for safety, economic viability, and timely project completion. Committed to these principles, we consistently deliver solutions that exceed client expectations.

    Areas of Expertise

    Structural Assessment

    Proficient in structural evaluation of concrete, masonry, steel, and timber structures, utilizing both analytical and experimental techniques to ensure a comprehensive assesment.

    Rehabilitation and Strengthening Expertise

    Specialized in analysis and design for the rehabilitation and strengthening of both historic and modern structures, including monuments, with a focus on preserving structural integrity.

    Seismic Risk Analysis

    Expertise in conducting seismic risk analysis of buildings and lifelines, employing advanced methodologies to assess and mitigate multihazard vulnerabilities.

    Fire-Struck Building Evaluation

    Adept at the evaluation and strengthening of buildings affected by fire, implementing strategic measures to restore and enhance structural stability.

    Base Isolation Design and Rehabilitation

    Skilled in the design and rehabilitation of structures using base isolation techniques, ensuring resilience and minimizing the impact of seismic forces.

    Wind Turbine Foundation Design

    Specialized in the design and rehabilitation of wind turbine foundations, combining engineering expertise to ensure stability and optimal performance in varying wind conditions.
